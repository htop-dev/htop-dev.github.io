Mailing list

To be informed of new releases, subscribe to the htop mailing list:

You can subscribe by sending an email to htop+subscribe@groups.io with "subscribe" in the subject.
Likewise, to unsubscribe send a message to htop+unsubscribe@groups.io with "unsubscribe" in the subject.

You can also search the list archive.

More information at the htop mailing list page, Groups.io. page.
